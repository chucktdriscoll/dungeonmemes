WIZARD TOWER



The tower appears like a normal, well constructed tower from the outside. Within all seems fairly normal as well, to the casual observer, at least till you starting going up several levels. Careful observation may reveal that the inner dimensions don’t quite seem to match up with the outside. The tower actually links multiple towers around the world, sort of stacked atop each other. The one in town is the ‘bottom’, going down leads to the basement and there no further stairs down from there. Going up, however, you’ll soon find that you can go up more levels than should fit. The levels immediately above the tower the crew has seen so far are ‘in’ a tower in a major city (as yet unnamed) in the same country. Levels further up lead to other towers (not yet defined). The properties of this tower are the result of an Artifact that Trevor discovered some time ago. This is not the primary function of the Artifact, simply a side-effect or secondary power (full scope of the Artifact’s power have not been defined). It seems likely that Trevor does not know, or at least cannot access the primary powers of the Artifact at this time.
